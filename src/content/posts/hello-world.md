I've been meaning to write more for a while. Long-form thinking is a different muscle than the rapid-fire decisions of building things, and lately I've been doing a lot of the latter and not enough of the former.

So: a blog. No grand plan, no posting schedule. Just a place to put down what I'm working through.

## What you'll find here

Mostly notes from the edges of where I'm working — AI agents, tooling, the architecture of personal computing, the systems we build to think with. Sometimes opinions. Occasionally something useful.

## What you won't

A content calendar. A newsletter funnel. Hot takes on whatever's trending. If the post took ten minutes to think about, it probably won't show up here.

That's it. More soon.
