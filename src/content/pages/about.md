I'm Mumen — engineer, builder, perpetually curious about how tools shape thought.

Most of what I work on these days lives at the intersection of AI agents and personal computing — building systems that augment how I think, write, and ship. I care about restraint in design: small blast radius, plugin over fork, MVP over full-version. Single sources of truth over ten places where the same fact slowly drifts apart.

This blog is a place to write down what I learn along the way.
